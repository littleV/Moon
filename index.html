It's a company that brings art to daily life.

What do we do?

We build an app that brings art to daily life. It's an art exchange that helps emerging artists to build a fan base, sell their art, and helps a user to discover the latest trend of the art world.

Why do we do this?

The founder is an artist. When she started out as an artist, she found out marketing for emerging artists was really hard and expensive. Then she came up with this idea to use AI to market art to audiences with low cost. We believe art is for everyone. With mass access of smart phones, we believe art can reach more audiences.
